NANO SATELLITE SOLUTIONS
FOR THE KENYAN MARKET

We are a consortium of Kenyan University Students seeking to provide a nanosatellite solution to solve some of Kenya's most challenging problems. Our 3U Nanosatellite, equipped with specialized optical payloads is a chance for Kenya to be a market leader in new frontiers. Our missions are listed below
01
SOLAR MAPPING

To generate a detailed solar profile of Kenya to provide a bankable solar radiation dataset for Solar Industry players

02
CARBON TRADING

Monitor Forest cover in Kenya using the 3U nanosatellite over time to create a platform for carbon trading with various entities such as companies and foreign governments as well as identify illegal logging.

03
CROP YIELD

This mission will facilitate monitoring of crop health and yield estimation using the 3U nanosatellite over time.

04
URBAN PLANNING

Utilize the nanosatellite solution for a wide range of urban planning applications such as mapping and land monitoring of urban areas.

05
IED DETECTION

Utilize the nanosatellite solution for detection of Improvised Explosive Devices.
